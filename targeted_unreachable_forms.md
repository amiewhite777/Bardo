# Targeted Questions for 7 Unreachable Forms

- a. Watch carefully to identify who has power and resources (The Wolf, 5 pts)
- b. Quickly assess who might be useful allies or threats (The Shark, 2 pts)

- d. Observe quietly to understand the social dynamics (The Snake, 2 pts)
- e. Gradually warm up once you feel comfortable (The Rabbit, 2 pts)
- f. Engage confidently and make your presence known (The Lion, 2 pts)
